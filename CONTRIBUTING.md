# Contributing

AI Workroot is early. The current focus is the protocol, structure, and philosophy.

Good contributions:

- make the first-use experience simpler
- improve the architecture language
- identify missing abstractions
- propose task and Mind templates
- propose personal examples
- improve portability and privacy guidance
- improve helper scripts without making them mandatory infrastructure

If you want a concrete starter task, see `docs/good-first-issues.md`.

If you want to understand what kinds of contributors the project needs, see `docs/who-we-are-looking-for.md`.

For practical user workflows, see `docs/user-sop.md`.

## Who We Hope To Work With

AI Workroot is philosophy-led and engineering-grounded.

We especially welcome exceptional individual contributors who bring expert-level strength in at least one of three areas:

- philosophical depth: you think deeply about human continuity, agency, memory, knowledge, responsibility, privacy, release, love, and growth in the age of AI
- AI engineering excellence: you can turn abstract principles into simple, rigorous, maintainable, portable systems across agents, models, runtimes, memory, indexes, and extension boundaries
- consumer AI product judgment: you understand how ordinary people start, trust, and keep using AI products without needing to understand their internal architecture

The strongest contributors combine all three. This project is not looking only for more code or scattered roles. It is looking for people who can help protect the spirit of the project while making the implementation and product experience stronger.

Good future maintainers should be able to:

- keep the person as the subject
- avoid turning AI Workroot into a tool-centered framework
- simplify the first-use experience for ordinary people
- design protocols that survive across agents, models, and operating systems
- build carefully without adding unnecessary complexity
- preserve privacy, portability, and user ownership
- disagree thoughtfully and improve the project through clear reasoning

If this describes you and you want to help maintain AI Workroot over the long term, contact:

```text
aiworkroot@protonmail.com
```

For bugs, small improvements, and public discussion, prefer GitHub Issues or Discussions when available.

Use the GitHub issue templates when they are available:

- documentation improvement
- feature request
- use case

Small, focused pull requests are easier to review than broad rewrites.

Avoid for now:

- heavy frameworks
- provider-specific lock-in
- mandatory servers
- mandatory databases
- domain-specific assumptions in the core

## Principle

Keep AI Workroot human-centered.

Agents, models, skills, MCP servers, plugins, databases, and indexes are tools. They must serve the subject's continuity, not replace it.
