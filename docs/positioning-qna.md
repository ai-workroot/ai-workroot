# Positioning Q&A

## What Is AI Workroot?

AI Workroot is a personal, local-first Workroot for individuals.

It is a user-owned continuity foundation for working with AI across tools.

AI Workroot asks:

> When a person uses many AI agents, models, tools, and clients over time, where should that person's context, progress, decisions, memory, and continuity live?

Its answer is:

> They should belong to the person's own Workroot.

Agents may change.
Models may change.
Platforms may change.
Tools may change.

The person's long-term continuity should not be locked inside any single tool.

---

## Is AI Workroot An Agent?

No.

AI Workroot is not an agent.

It is a personal, local-first Workroot that agents can work around.

Agents execute tasks.

AI Workroot preserves continuity.

---

## Is AI Workroot A Coding Tool?

No.

Coding agents can work inside a Workroot, but AI Workroot is not limited to code.

A Workroot can support writing, learning, planning, research, personal projects, life organization, entrepreneurship, or software development.

---

## Is AI Workroot A Memory System?

Not exactly.

Many AI memory systems ask:

> How can AI remember the user better?

AI Workroot asks:

> How can the user own their continuity?

Memory is only one part of continuity.

AI Workroot also cares about decisions, progress, handoff, invalidation, intentional forgetting, and user ownership.

---

## Is AI Workroot Just A Folder?

No.

At first glance, AI Workroot may look like a folder because the public seed is file-first.

But an ordinary folder mainly answers:

> Where are the files?

AI Workroot tries to answer:

> What is this person continuing here?

> How should AI continue next time?

> How does this continuity remain owned by the person?

The current file layout is an implementation shape. The deeper purpose is continuity.

---

## How Is AI Workroot Related To Agents Like Hermes?

Hermes is a strong example of a long-running agent runtime.

It can have memory, skills, tools, MCP, gateways, automation, and long-running behavior.

AI Workroot is different.

Hermes is agent-centric.

AI Workroot is person-centric.

Hermes may work inside a Workroot.

But the Workroot belongs to the person.

The stronger agents become, the more important it becomes for the person to own the continuity layer.

---

## Does AI Workroot Replace Existing AI Tools?

No.

AI Workroot should work with existing and future AI tools.

An agent can enter a Workroot, understand context, help complete work, and leave a clear handoff for the next session or the next agent.

The best relationship is complementary:

- agents do the work
- Workroot preserves the meaning, state, and handoff of the work
- the user owns the foundation

---

## Why Personal-First?

Because the core problem is personal continuity.

One person may use many agents, models, tools, and devices over time.

That person may have unfinished tasks, long-term goals, changing circumstances, old decisions, lessons learned, and things they no longer want to carry.

AI Workroot gives that person a stable foundation.

Team collaboration may become a separate direction in the future, but it is not the core product now.

---

## Why Local-First?

Because ownership matters.

A person's long-term context, progress, decisions, memory, and source files should not be completely locked inside a remote product.

Local-first does not mean isolated.

It means the user owns the foundation.

The user should be able to inspect it, move it, back it up, and decide what to preserve or put down.

AI Workroot may connect with future cloud services and agent ecosystems.

But its foundation should belong to the user.

---

## What Is Clean Mode?

Clean Mode is a future user-facing direction where the visible user directory stays clean and Workroot state is managed separately.

AI needs structure.

But ordinary users should not be forced to see or manage that structure.

Complexity should serve the user, not expose itself to the user.

---

## Why Does AI Workroot Care About Forgetting?

AI should not only help people remember.

Human life also needs release.

Some experiences should leave a lesson, a decision, a principle, or a quiet marker without remaining active in every future session.

AI Workroot treats forgetting, invalidation, release, and tombstones as part of responsible continuity.

The person should decide what remains active, what becomes quiet, and what can be put down.

---

## One Sentence

AI Workroot is a personal, local-first Workroot for individuals, where different AI agents can understand context, continue progress, hand off work, and help people manage meaningful work over time.

Shorter:

> AI agents may come and go. Your Workroot stays with you.
