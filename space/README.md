# Space

This is the user-visible workspace.

You can put your own work, files, notes, projects, and outputs here. You may add your own folders under `space/` when they help your real work.

Some folders are protocol anchors and should keep their names:

- `profile/`: who or what this Workroot serves
- `work/`: user-visible outputs, reports, and summaries
- `mind/`: long-term memory, knowledge, principles, decisions, and release markers
- `inbox/`: rough capture before classification
- `files/`: source materials and references

You do not need to manage internal task records, indexes, caches, or kernel files. Say what you want to do; the AI agent should organize the work behind the scenes.
