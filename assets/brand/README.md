# Brand Assets

## Current Status

AI Workroot does not currently include a final logo or visual image.

The brand direction is still being explored. The future visual identity should express a real human standing before the sea and stars, preparing to move toward their own direction with AI as a long-term collaborator.

The image should feel real, calm, open, and hopeful. It should not feel like a cartoon mascot or a technology-first logo.

Core symbols:

- person: the human remains the subject
- beach: the place before departure
- sea: life, work, uncertainty, and open possibility
- stars: dreams, direction, and long-term aspiration
- horizon: the future that asks for courage and action

## Usage

Do not add temporary or unfinished visual assets to the public release.

## Direction

Future brand work should keep the human and the horizon central.

Avoid modifying the identity into a technology-first mark. AI Workroot is about people, memory, work, love, growth, and long-term continuity.

Do not use third-party or generated photographic material as a registered trademark without a separate legal review.
