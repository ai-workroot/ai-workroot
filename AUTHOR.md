# Author

AI Workroot was created by an independent builder using the public identity of this project.

The creator intentionally keeps personal identity in the background. The project should be judged by its ideas, usefulness, and long-term value, not by biography, title, company, or social label.

## Creator's Perspective

The creator tends to stand outside a problem before entering it.

Instead of only solving the visible task, the first instinct is to ask:

- What is the real problem?
- What is the deeper structure behind it?
- Which parts are accidental, and which parts are essential?
- Can this be abstracted into something useful for more people?
- After the abstraction is clear, what is the simplest practical thing to do next?

AI Workroot comes from that way of thinking.

It begins with a philosophical question about how people should live and work in the age of AI, but it must become something practical enough for a person to clone, open, and use.

## Values

The project reflects a few steady values:

- See the essence of a problem without escaping from reality.
- Think at a high level, then return to concrete execution.
- Help ordinary people gain real leverage from AI.
- Respect human judgment, memory, values, and growth.
- Preserve lessons without forcing people to carry old pain as active identity.
- Prefer clarity over noise.
- Prefer continuity over scattered fragments.
- Prefer openness over lock-in.
- Build with kindness, responsibility, and love.
- Hope that every person can carry love in their heart, that the world can remain peaceful, and that humanity can endure.

## Relationship To The Project

AI Workroot is not meant to become a personal monument.

The author's role is to start the structure, express the values, and keep the project honest:

- human first
- AI as collaborator
- work as the path to results
- memory as the path to continuity
- knowledge as the path to growth
- portability as the protection of freedom

Over time, the project should belong to everyone who uses it, improves it, and makes it part of their own work and life.
